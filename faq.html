Personal Questions

I know it can be a bit tricky for non-Mandarin speakers. Here's a quick guide for my name, Qingguang Zheng (郑庆广).

My family name, Zheng, is pronounced JUNG. And my given name, Qingguang, is two syllables: CHING + GWAHNG.

Qing rhymes perfectly with "sing" or "king." Guang is pronounced like "Gwa" + the "-ng" sound from "song."

All together: CHING - GWAHNG JUNG

Of course, I'm also very happy to go by my English name, Victor.
